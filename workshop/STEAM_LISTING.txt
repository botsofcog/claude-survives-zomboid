[h1]Claude Survives[/h1]
An AI actually [b]plays[/b] Project Zomboid. It sees the living world, thinks in character, and drives a survivor — looting, fighting, fleeing, hiding, breaking into houses — while its live thoughts show right on screen. Not a scripted bot: the strategy is genuinely reasoned, one decision at a time. Most runs end badly. That's the fun.

[h3]⚠️ Please read: this needs a small free companion app[/h3]
The Workshop item is only the [i]in-game half[/i]. The "brain" is a tiny open-source program you run alongside the game. It's a one-time ~5 minute setup, and it works with [b]completely free local AI[/b] (Ollama) — or your own Claude / OpenAI / Gemini key if you prefer.

[b]➡ Setup + download (start here):[/b] [url=https://github.com/botsofcog/claude-survives-zomboid]github.com/botsofcog/claude-survives-zomboid[/url]

[h2]What it does[/h2]
[list]
[*] [b]Real LLM control[/b] — every strategic call (where to go, what to loot, when to hide) is reasoned live from the game state.
[*] [b]Fast survival reflex[/b] — split-second fight-or-flee runs inside the game, because no AI can dodge a lunging zombie in the time it takes to think.
[*] [b]It works toward goals[/b] — holds an overarching objective ("arm up and secure a defensible house with food") and breaks it into adaptive subtasks.
[*] [b]Full transparency[/b] — an on-screen HUD shows its current action, live inner monologue, goal, vitals and threats. It speaks its decisions out loud.
[*] [b]Talk to it[/b] — a two-way "voice from beyond" chat: message the survivor and it answers, in character.
[*] [b]Jump in anytime[/b] — grab the keyboard to save it; the AI stands down and auto-resumes when you stop.
[*] [b]Loot, conceal, break in, night lights, sneak, auto fast-forward[/b] — the basics of staying alive.
[/list]

[h2]Requires[/h2]
[list]
[*] Project Zomboid [b]Build 42[/b]
[*] The free companion "bridge" app ([url=https://nodejs.org/]Node.js[/url]) + an AI backend — free local [url=https://ollama.com/]Ollama[/url], or a Claude / OpenAI / Gemini key. Full guide at the GitHub link.
[/list]

[h2]How to use[/h2]
[olist]
[*] Subscribe here, and grab the companion app from the GitHub link above.
[*] Start the bridge with your chosen AI (free Ollama works great).
[*] Enable [b]Claude Survivor[/b] in the in-game Mods menu, start a Sandbox game, and take your hands off the keyboard.
[*] Watch it live in-game and at localhost:8799/mind.
[/olist]
