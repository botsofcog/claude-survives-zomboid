description=[list]

description=[*] A fast in-game reflex layer handles moment-to-moment survival (fight / flee / dodge / close doors) — because no AI can dodge a zombie in the time it takes to think.
description=[*] On-screen HUD shows its current action, live inner monologue, vitals, and threats. It speaks its decisions aloud.

description=[*] Auto fast-forwards safe travel, snaps to normal near danger.
