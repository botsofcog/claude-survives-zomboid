It sees the living world, thinks in character, and drives a survivor — looting, fighting, fleeing, hiding, breaking into houses — while its live thoughts show on screen. Not a scripted bot: the strategy is genuinely reasoned, one decision at a time. Most runs end badly. That's the fun.

description=[h2]What it does[/h2]
description=[list]
description=[*] [b]Real LLM control[/b] — every strategic call is reasoned live from the game state.
description=[*] [b]Fast survival reflex[/b] — split-second fight-or-flee inside the game, because no AI can dodge a lunging zombie in the time it takes to think.
description=[*] [b]Works toward goals[/b] — holds an overarching objective and breaks it into adaptive subtasks.
description=[*] [b]Full transparency[/b] — on-screen HUD shows its action, live inner monologue, goal, vitals and threats. It speaks its decisions out loud.
description=[*] [b]Talk to it[/b] — a two-way chat: message the survivor and it answers, in character.
description=[*] [b]Jump in anytime[/b] — grab the keyboard to save it; the AI auto-resumes when you stop.
description=[*] [b]Loot, conceal, break in, night lights, sneak, auto fast-forward[/b] — the basics of staying alive.
